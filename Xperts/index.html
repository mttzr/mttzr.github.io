Step 1: Meet your driver

Once you're approved as an Xpert, just go online as normal and you'll begin to receive Xpert requests.

After accepting an Xpert request, drive to the designated location and park nearby to meet your new driver.

FYI: New drivers have just signed up and are counting on your help! They should be waiting for you next to their car with their vehicle documents.

Next

Step 2: Perform a quick quality check

Now, the app will gide you through a vehicle checklist to make sure your new driver's care is safe and ready to go.

PRO TIP: Safety and quality are important, so complete the checklist accurately as possible. You can always edit your answers on the main vehicle checklist screen.

Next

Exterior Check

First, please check the outside of the car to make sure it is in good condition.
Don't worry, we'll show you a series of step-by-step instructions.

You'll be checking:

  • Headlights
  • Taillights
  • Brake Lights
  • Turn Indicators
  • Tires
  • Bumpers
  • Windows
  • Side Mirrors
  • Doors
  • Windshield and Wipers
Next

Interior Check
(take a test drive!)

The last half of your vehicle checklist will be completed from the passenger's seat. Here, you'll ask the new driver to take a quick 10-minute test drive.
Have them drive around the area so you can see how their car moves on the road, and don't forget to buckle up!

You'll Be Checking:

  • Seats
  • Speedometer
  • Safety Belts
  • Brakes
  • Rearview Mirror
  • Emergency Brake
  • Wheel and Horn
Next

Checking tires

IMPORTANT: Tires can be tricky, so you'll want to make sure all 4 tires are in good condition and not too worn out, with at least 2/32' of tread depth.
You can use the penny test to check tread depth. Stick a penny down into a groove of the tire. If part of Lincoln's head is covered the tread is deep enough.

Take a profile photo

Your new driver needs a friendly new photo for their account, so riders can see who they are when requesting rides.

Don't zoom in or out too much, from the neck up is best.

Set up the Uber Partner App

Almost done! Now, just help set up the Partner app for your new driver.
First, ask your new driver if they already downloaded the app. If they did, ask them to open it and show you.
If they don't have it, please follow the steps on your screen to help them get the app.
