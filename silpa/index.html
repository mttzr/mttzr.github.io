Silpa Kovvali is a New York based writer who focuses on social and cultural criticism. Her work has appeared in Al Jazeera America, The Atlantic, Feministing, The Huffington Post, The New Republic, The Point, Refinery29, The Root, Salon, and Thought Catalog and she has appeared on CNN Headline News. She graduated from Harvard College in 2010 with a degree in Computer Science and received her MBA from Wharton in 2015.
